Welcome to our Platform Saitwal, where we are committed to improving the lives of individuals and families in our community. Our program is designed to address a range of needs, including education, health, social welfare, and economic development. We believe that by empowering our community members, we can create a more just and equitable society where everyone has the opportunity to thrive. Through our initiatives and partnerships with local organizations, we are working to build a stronger and more resilient community, one that is grounded in the principles of compassion, respect, and social responsibility.

Educational Scholarships

Education is a fundamental aspect of personal growth and community development. At our organization, we recognize the value of education and the barriers that many individuals face in accessing it. As part of our community development program, we are proud to offer educational scholarships to deserving students who may not have the financial means to pursue their academic goals.

Our scholarships are open to students of all ages, and fields of study. We believe that education should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their socioeconomic status. Through our scholarship program, we hope to support and encourage students who have the potential to make a positive impact on their communities and the world.

Our scholarships are awarded based on a variety of factors, including academic achievement, financial need, and community involvement. We are further enabling a comprehensive support to our scholarship recipients, including mentorship and networking opportunities, to help them achieve their academic and professional goals.

By providing access to education, we hope to create a more equitable and prosperous society for all.

Employment support

At our organization, we recognize that education is the foundation for success, and we are committed to providing our community members with the resources and guidance they need to achieve their professional goals. That is why we have established a Career Guidance Cell, a dedicated program aimed at providing career guidance and support to our community members.

The Career Guidance Cell is designed to help individuals at every stage of their professional journey, from choosing the right educational path to finding meaningful employment. Our team of experienced professional’s works with community members to assess their skills and interests, identify potential career paths, and provide guidance on the education and training needed to pursue those paths.

We offer a range of services and resources, including workshops on resume writing, job searching, and interviewing skills, as well as one-on-one career counseling sessions. Our goal is to equip our community members with the tools and knowledge they need to succeed in their chosen careers.

We believe that with the right guidance and support, anyone can achieve their professional goals, and we are committed to helping our community members do just that.

Arts & Culture

At our organization, we believe that the arts play a vital role in promoting and preserving cultural heritage, fostering creativity and innovation, and bringing people together. Our program is designed to celebrate and showcase the diverse cultural traditions of our community, while also providing opportunities for individuals to explore their own creativity.

Through our Arts and Culture program, we offer a range of events, workshops, and performances that showcase the rich artistic traditions of our community, including music, dance, theater, literature, and visual arts. We also provide support and resources for artists and creatives in our community, including mentorship.

Our program is open to all members of the community, regardless of age. Whether you are a seasoned artist or a beginner, we welcome you to join us and explore your creativity.

So come and join us in celebrating the arts and culture of our community! We look forward to seeing you at our upcoming events and workshops.

Jain Pathshala

We have been investing in religious education, training the next generation. While in many places, Jain young people most often live in religiously saturated environments, allowing them to acquire knowledge of their religion through daily life by interacting with the monks, temples, texts, and people that surround them. However, due to urbanisation, not all get the same advantage. Here Jains are spread too thinly to provide young people with the Jain environment necessary to sustain such education by osmosis. In addition, the assurance of accessible resources is replaced by the formidable barriers of social media distraction.

What do the teachers, who are almost always parents, feel is important to pass on? What role do culture and language play in religious education? How does the pathshala balance elements such as ritual and philosophy? How can it help children to apply a Jain perspective to daily life? How can children learn reading Jinwani and so on.

For your specific needs, or if you are interested in starting a pathshala, please get in touch with us and we will set up a discussion.
